Here's how to make Black Coffee

Here's how to make a delicious cup of Black Coffee that's sure to rejanuvate you and get your day started in the best way possible

Here's the steps:

  1. Fill a boiling pan with needed amount of water and start heating
  2. Add a teaspoon of instant coffee to your cup
  3. Add required amount of sugar depending on your preference
  4. When the water comes to a boil, add a very small amount of water to create a concentrated mixture of water, coffee and sugar
  5. Stir until it becomes gooey
  6. Add remaining water to dilute the mixture
  7. Enjoy
